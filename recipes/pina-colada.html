Pina Colada

ingredients

  • 120ml pineapple juice
  • 60ml white rum
  • 60ml coconut cream
  • pineappe wedge

Instructions

  1. pulse the pineapple juice, white rum, and coconut cream in a blender with ice until smooth
  2. pour into a tall glass and add the pineapple wedge for garnish
  3. server and enjoy
