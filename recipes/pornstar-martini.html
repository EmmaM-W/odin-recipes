Pornstar Martini

ingredients

  • 2 ripe passion fruits
  • 60ml of vanilla vodka
  • 30ml of passoa
  • 1tbsp of lime juice
  • 1tbsp of sugar syrup

Instructions

  1. cut one of the passion fruit in half and scoop the seeds into a cocktail shaker
  2. add the vodka, passoa, lime juice, and sugar syrup to the shaker with a handful of crushed ice
  3. shake well
  4. strain into two martini glasses
  5. cut the remaining passion fruit in half and add a half into each glass
  6. serve and enjoy
